Instructions

Step 1: Check Your Balances

Review the amounts that you have left in your Health Care Savings Account or Lifestyle Savings Account (if applicable); you can do this by logging into your Great West Life and/or League account.



Remember that your remaining balance on December 31st will carry over to the following year and will be available for 1 year.

Step 2: Plan For Your Upcoming Expenses

Think about the claims that you or your dependents may have in the upcoming calendar year for services not covered under our basic health and dental plans. Some examples to consider:

Health Care Spending Account

Glasses or contact lenses
Orthodontics
Prescription Drugs

Lifestyle Spending Account

Gym Membership
Daycare Costs
Transit/Parking

Step 3: Estimate Your Amounts

Using the calculator below, you can estimate the amounts you wish to contribute to your accounts.


Step 4: Enter Amounts in Workday

Once you've determined the amounts you want to allot, enter them in Workday.
